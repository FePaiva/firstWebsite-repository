Hi there! Nice to meet you! I am Brasil!

I would like to share a few things about me, so you can know me a bit better.

I was discovered by the Portugueses in 1500, when Pedro Alvares Cabral, who was trying to reach India, landed in my shores. When the Portuguese Crown found out how rich I was, they decided to stay and have me as one of their colonies.

My name, Brasil, comes from a tree called Pau Brasil. The word “brasil”, derives from “brasa”(ember), in reference to the red color of the tree’s trunk associated with fire.
